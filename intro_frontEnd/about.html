Where we started

It all began back in 1940, we were at war
with the Nazi Germany.

All the trenches were full of soldiers
who were tired and they were starving.

Our luck was the arrival of the Americans,
they came to our salvation, and helped us
win this goddamn war.

I hope someday I'll be able to
forget those horrors...
